In this movie we see someone mastrubating. We just see some bodyparts, no genitalia were shown during the video. It was my choice to do this so the spectator doesn't know what he sees in the beginning. By the contraction of the muscles, the spectator can derive that the person in the video is mastrubating. The quality of the video is a bit pixelated. This gives the video a trash content. I like to work with this trash/porn contents, for me it works coherent. I describe my work as trashporn.
